About Us


Our team of professionals offers extensive experience in portfolio management, asset allocation, market analysis, and risk management. From our offices in Boston, MA, we manage assets for institutional and qualified individual investors, worldwide.



Portfolio Management
Asset Allocation

Risk Management

Three partners launched P/E Investments in 1995 from a barn in Weston, MA. Today we serve qualified individual and institutional investors, worldwide. Our absolute return strategies leverage extensive experience in portfolio management, asset allocation, market analysis and risk management to invest globally on a long/short basis. Our style is quantitative, based on fundamental macroeconomic and financial factors, but may include qualitative perspectives as well.
